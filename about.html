Our Mission


Our mission is both to entertain and to start a conversation. The film is a mix between fantasy entertainment and a personal story about cooping mechanism and fear disorder. In the first place we hope that our product connects with people that are going through something similar. That they feel understood. Secondly this film is for their loved once, so that they can have an insight in what this other person is going through. A Peculiar Passage towards a better understanding of the human heart and mind.

A Peculiar Passage

A Peculiar Passage plays in our modern world that is inhabited by fantastical creatures such as elves and trolls, rather than humans. The young Peter is on a mission to defeat his arch enemy Tevo Nera with the help of his trusted advisor Itherpast. However, when he finally overcomes the obstacles put in his way and marches into his final battle, Peter is faced with a life-changing challenge that he couldn’t have been prepared for.
